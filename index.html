Hello pilot, welcome to the Boys und Flugzueg website!

About us

We are a group of Girls und Panzer enthusiasts who felt that the series had room for expansion (And not in the conventional fanfiction manner).

Girls in tanks was a pretty cool concept, then we got thinking...
"What if there were boys in planes too?"
"That would be pretty cool."

Our collective group consists of regular people like yourself, people who have crazy ideas and a hankering for more content out of our favorite animes.
We are students, workers, wage slaves and freeloaders who wanted to put out a collective piece of artwork that an entire community of people can enjoy.

This project has been a large part of our lives, dragging us through thick and thin, highs and lows.
Through constant perseverance (And a collective council of leaders) we have managed to slam together what we believe to be our groups best work.

These past few years have been challenging for all involved so we implore you, our readers, to give us some feedback on our discord server, it'd be pretty nice if you could.

I hope you enjoy your stay and our work.
