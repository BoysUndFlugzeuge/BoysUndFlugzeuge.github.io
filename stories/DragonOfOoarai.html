Dragon of Oarai

Information

After causing an accident in the Sentouki-do finals, Ryu Morimoto is expelled from his former school of St. Gloriana. Oarai, however, gives him another chance to continue flying. Ryu must now lead his new team up the national tournament ladder to help save their school and show St. Gloriana that expelling him was a mistake.
